SPIFlash API

High-Level Groups

  • Initialization: begin, setClock, sfdpPresent

  • Introspection: getJEDECID, getUniqueID, getCapacity

  • Addressing: getAddress, sizeofStr

  • Read/write primitives and arrays

  • Typed and generic I/O: writeAnything/readAnything

  • Erase operations

  • Power and suspend/resume operations

Behavioral Notes

  • Writes are page-aware and can span page boundaries

  • Optional error-check verification is enabled by default

  • Erase timing may be SFDP-derived or library defaults
